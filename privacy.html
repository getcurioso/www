Privacy Policy — Curio TCG

How Curio TCG collects, uses and protects personal data

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Curio TCG ("Curio TCG", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores and shares information when you use our mobile applications, website and related services (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is available at the project website and via our mobile apps.

By using the Service you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

Data Controller & Contact

Data Controller: Curio TCG
Contact: support@curiotcg.app

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: such as username, email address (if provided), and password.
  • Profile & Collection Data: cards you add to your collection or wishlist, binders you create, notes, card conditions, and other details you choose to store.
  • Marketplace & Social Features: listings you create, messages you send to other users, likes or interactions with binders, and other user-generated content.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: interactions with the app or website, such as pages visited, features used, and in-app actions.
  • Device and Technical Data: device type, operating system, app version, language, and approximate region.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies (Website): we may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze traffic, remember preferences, and improve your experience.

2.3 Analytics

Our website may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or similar services) to understand how visitors use the site. These tools collect information such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and general device information. This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

2.4 Camera & Media Access

The Curio TCG mobile app may request permission to access your device camera and/or photos/media when you choose to take or upload card images. Access is optional and only used for the purposes described below.

  • Taking photos: to capture card images directly from the app for adding to your collection or listings.
  • Uploading photos: to select existing images from your device to attach to your collection items or marketplace listings.

Images you choose to upload are treated as User Content. Uploaded images may be stored on our servers or a third-party content delivery network (CDN) to provide the Service, and may be resized or processed for performance and display purposes. You control the visibility of uploaded images through your account settings and can request deletion by contacting support@curiotcg.app.

Camera and media permissions are only used when you explicitly grant them and perform an action that requires them. Curio TCG does not collect camera images or media in the background and does not access your device photos unless you initiate an upload or take a photo within the app. You can revoke permissions at any time via your device settings.

Third-party providers (for example, CDN or moderation services) may process uploaded images as part of hosting, delivery or moderation; see the "How We Share Your Information" section for details about third-party processors.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining the Curio TCG Service.
  • Allowing you to manage your card collection, binders, wishlist, and graded cards.
  • Enabling marketplace features such as listing cards for sale and contacting other collectors.
  • Improving and personalizing your experience within the app and website.
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the Service and user accounts.
  • Communicating with you about updates, changes, or important notices related to the Service.
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (If Applicable)

Depending on your location, we may rely on various legal bases to process your personal data, including:

  • Your consent, when required.
  • Performance of a contract (providing the Service you requested).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Our legitimate interests, such as improving the Service and preventing abuse.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: third-party services that help us operate the Service, such as hosting, analytics, or error monitoring. These providers only process data on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations.
  • With Other Users: information you choose to make public (e.g., public binders, public profile, marketplace listings) may be visible to other users.
  • For Legal Reasons: if required by law, regulation, or legal request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety and that of our users.
  • Business Transfers: in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. If you delete your account, we will take reasonable steps to remove or anonymize your personal data, except where retention is required by law or justified by legitimate interests (for example, security logs or fraud prevention).

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Export your data in a structured, commonly used format (where technically feasible).
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent (this does not affect prior lawful processing).

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the Contact section below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Curio TCG is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law (for example, 13 or 16 years old, depending on jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
