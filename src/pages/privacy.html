Privacy Policy — Curio TCG

How Curio TCG collects, uses and protects personal data

Last Updated: 2026-03-19

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Curio TCG ("Curio TCG", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores and shares information when you use our mobile applications, website and related services (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is available at the project website and via our mobile apps.

By using the Service you accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

Data Controller & Contact

Data Controller: Curio TCG
Contact: support@curiotcg.app

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: such as username, email address, and password.
  • Third-Party Authentication Data: If you choose to register or log in using a third-party service (such as Google or Apple/iCloud), we collect certain information provided by those services, including your name, email address, and the unique identifier (ID) used to authenticate your account.
  • Profile & Collection Data: cards you add to your collection or wishlist, binders you create, notes, card conditions, and other details you choose to store.
  • Marketplace & Social Features: listings you create, messages you send to other users, likes or interactions with binders, and other user-generated content.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: interactions with the app or website, such as pages visited, features used, and in-app actions.
  • Device and Technical Data: device type, operating system, app version, language, and approximate region.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies (Website): we may use cookies and similar technologies to analyze traffic, remember preferences, and improve your experience.

2.3 Analytics

Our website and mobile applications may use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar services) to understand how visitors use the Service. These tools collect information such as pages visited, time spent, and general device information. This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

2.4 Camera & Media Access

The Curio TCG mobile app may request permission to access your device camera and/or photos/media when you choose to take or upload card images.

  • Taking photos: to capture card images directly from the app for adding to your collection or listings.
  • Uploading photos: to select existing images from your device.
  • Note: Curio TCG does not collect camera images or media in the background. You can revoke permissions at any time via your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing, operating, and maintaining the Curio TCG Service.
  • Authenticating your identity via password, Google, or Apple login.
  • Allowing you to manage your card collection, binders, and wishlist.
  • Enabling marketplace features and communication between collectors.
  • Improving and personalizing your experience.
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the Service.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Depending on your location, we may rely on:

  • Your consent.
  • Performance of a contract (providing the Service you requested).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Our legitimate interests (improving the Service and preventing abuse).

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With Service Providers: third-party services (hosting, analytics, error monitoring).
  • With Authentication Providers: When you use Google or Apple login, certain data is shared between us and the provider to facilitate the login process.
  • With Other Users: Information you choose to make public (public binders, marketplace listings).
  • For Legal Reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Service.

Account Deletion: You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can initiate this process:

  1. Through the Settings menu within the Curio TCG mobile app.
  2. By contacting us at support@curiotcg.app.

Upon deletion, we will take reasonable steps to remove or anonymize your personal data, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Export your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@curiotcg.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Curio TCG is not intended for children under the minimum age required by law (e.g., 13 or 16). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Transfers

The Service may be operated from different countries. Your information may be stored and processed in regions other than your own. We take steps to ensure your data remains protected under applicable laws.

11. Play Store / App Store Data Safety

  • We collect certain personal and device data to provide features and ensure security.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Users can request data deletion directly in the app or via email.
  • Third-party providers (Google, Apple, Analytics) may receive limited data as described in this policy.

12. Contact

If you have any questions or requests to delete your data, contact us at:

Email: support@curiotcg.app
